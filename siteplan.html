WildXRuta – Website Plan

Site Plan for the WildXRuta Motorcycle Adventure Website

Site Name

WildXRuta represents a community of motorcycle enthusiasts who love exploring adventure routes and discovering new experiences. The name reflects freedom, exploration, and passion for the open road.

Optional domain: wildxruta.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of WildXRuta is to provide information about motorcycle adventures, including riding routes, safety and maintenance tips, and rider stories. The site will inspire people who love motorcycles or dream of starting their own adventure rides someday.

Scenarios

  • What are some of the best motorcycle routes in Guatemala for beginners?
  • What kind of motorcycle and gear do I need to start adventure riding safely?

Color Schema

The color palette uses red and gray tones to reflect energy, excitement, and strength — while maintaining a clean and modern look.

  • Primary Color: Deep Red (for headings, buttons, and highlights)
  • Secondary Color: Dark Gray (for text and accents)
  • Background: Light Gray (for backgrounds and cards)

Typography

  • Headings: Poppins – bold and clean for titles
  • Body Text: Open Sans – simple and readable for paragraphs

Wireframe

Basic wireframe layouts showing how the content will be structured.

Mobile View

  • Header with logo and hamburger menu
  • Hero image with title
  • Featured routes and adventure tips
  • Footer with contact and reference links

Desktop View

  • Header with horizontal navigation
  • Large hero banner
  • Three-column section: Routes | Tips | Blog
  • Footer with social links and reference page
